The Atumsoft Bridge

What is it?
The Atumsoft bridge is a small device that replaces the big, bulky computer attached to each instrument in a chemistry lab.

How does it work?
Instead of having a cable directly connected to a computer, the instrument is plugged into our bridge. This allows for all the data to be securely transmitted to any location that an analyst may be at from a laptop, tablet, or smartphone.

What are the benefits?
100% agnostic to the brand of instrument used
Allows labs to continue using the existing instrument software that they know and love
Greater control over lab instruments
Only analysts who are currently scheduled to work on an instrument can see it and access its data

How much is it?
FREE! We are planning to offer our bridge at no cost. You only have to pay a yearly licensing fee per employee. Please contact us for more information on pricing.
