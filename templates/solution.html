The Solution

What is it?
The Atumsoft bridge is a small device that replaces the big, bulky computer attached to each instrument in a chemistry lab.

How does it work?
Instead of having a cable directly connected to a computer, the instrument is plugged into our bridge. This allows for all the data to be securely transmitted to any location that an analyst may be at from a laptop, tablet, or smartphone.
