Our Product

At a time when wireless connectivity makes life easier and tasks more efficient in countless ways, most scientific labs are behind the curve. The problem is every machine is an island. It’s commonplace for scientists to operate cutting-edge instruments in not-so-cutting-edge ways.
Until now.

Atumsoft is a turnkey IoT device empowering any scientist to access any instrument from anywhere in wireless range. Thankfully, the time-intensive method of physically walking from instrument to instrument to run operations and collect data is obsolete. Atumsoft gives complete control, untethers access to data, and frees the scientist to focus on science.

What is it?

The Atumate Bridge is a small wireless device that attaches to all of your chemistry instruments.

How does it work?

The Atumate Bridge Plugs into any chemistry instrument, allowing scientists to run experiments anywhere in the world remotely.

What are the benefits?

  • Moniter everything from anywhere
  • Be more connected in your lab
  • Share information with co-workers with ease and more accuracy
  • Drive innovation by having your employees work smarter, thus being more productive
  • Save money by decreasing wasted time

Will it work for me?

  • 100% agnostic to the brand of the instrument
  • Allows labs to continue using the existing instrument software
  • Greater control over lab instruments
  • Only analysts who are currently scheduled to work on an instrument can see it and access its data.
